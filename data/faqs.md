## FAQs

### How much does a business website cost?
A smaller business website typically ranges from KES 60,000 to KES 120,000. Custom features, e-commerce, and AI integrations increase the budget.

### How long does it take to launch a website?
Most websites launch in 3 to 5 weeks. More complex applications and platforms take 8 to 12 weeks, depending on approvals and integrations.

### Can I add AI features to my website?
