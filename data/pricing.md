## Pricing

### Landing Page
Ideal for lead generation campaigns, event registration, and product launches.
Includes one conversion-focused page, mobile-first design, contact capture, and performance optimization.
Typical cost: KES 25,000–50,000
Timeline: 2–3 weeks
Recommended upgrades: SEO optimization, WhatsApp contact, analytics, maintenance.

### Business Website
Ideal for small businesses, consultancies, and professional services.
Includes homepage, service pages, about, contact, testimonials, and basic CMS support.
Typical cost: KES 60,000–120,000
Timeline: 3–5 weeks
Recommended upgrades: AI assistant, blog, multi-language support, CRM integration, ongoing maintenance.

### Corporate Website
Ideal for medium and large enterprises that need stronger brand positioning.
Includes custom sections, case studies, team pages, service funnels, and advanced performance.
Typical cost: KES 120,000–300,000
Timeline: 4–7 weeks
Recommended upgrades: video content, client portal, advanced analytics, SEO growth program.

### E-commerce Website
Ideal for retail, wholesale, and online product businesses.
Includes product catalog, checkout, payment gateway, inventory syncing, and order management.
Typical cost: KES 180,000–500,000
Timeline: 5–9 weeks
Recommended upgrades: AI shopping assistant, subscription sales, inventory automation, loyalty integrations.

### Web Application
Ideal for internal portals, booking systems, dashboards, and custom business workflows.
Includes user authentication, role-based access, data reporting, and integration with third-party services.
Typical cost: KES 250,000–450,000
Timeline: 8–12 weeks
Recommended upgrades: AI automation, business intelligence, payment integration, API connectivity.

### AI & Automation
Ideal for chatbots, process automation, intelligent workflows, and customer support systems.
Includes conversational AI, lead qualification, WhatsApp automation, and task routing.
Typical cost: KES 100,000–250,000
Timeline: 4–8 weeks
Recommended upgrades: CRM synchronization, analytics dashboards, voice assistant, chatbot knowledge base.
